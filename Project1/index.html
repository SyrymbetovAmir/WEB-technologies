Volkswagen Polo

This car proudly bears the legendary name. For 40 years now, Volkswagen Polo has been faithfully serving its owners, making life more convenient, more comfortable and more beautiful every day.

Features

Brand New Design

The Polo became the first model in Kazakhstan with the new Volkswagen logo. The exterior of the Volkswagen Polo is distinguished by an upgraded radiator grille, a new front bumper, rear diffuser, as well as completely updated LED optics.

Modern Engines

Volkswagen Polo is equipped with modern 1.6-liter engines, which are manufactured at the Kaluga plant and comply with the Euro-5 environmental standard. The power of the engines is 90 hp and 110 hp.

Comfort

The Volkswagen Polo is equipped with a new design steering wheel. Starting with the Status configuration, the steering wheel is multifunctional, leather-trimmed and has a heating function.
